Knees

Can’t play this? Listen directly.

"Pressure everywhere. Cold floorboards pressed up into her knees. His hand pressed on the back of her neck. The other hand gripped her hair, and the pull on the strands was a needle-clamp feeling on the scalp, an authority that made the rest of her limp as a kitten in its mother's jaw.

She looked up and his gaze pressed down against her skin like a weight. All of it held her in place while he used her.

His face was difficult to read - jaw set, eyes hard, but also… something. What the fuck was it? Sad, angry, excited? All those things…

The face of a man watching his house burn down, warming his hands against the flames..."
